Types of Waste We Handle

Learn about different waste categories and how we process them

Plastic Waste

Includes bottles, containers, packaging materials, and other plastic products.

  • Recyclable plastics (PET, HDPE, PVC)
  • Non-recyclable plastics
  • Plastic packaging materials
  • Single-use plastics

Green Waste

Organic waste from gardens, parks, and landscaping activities.

  • Grass clippings
  • Tree branches and leaves
  • Garden trimmings
  • Plant waste

Hard Waste

Large items that cannot be disposed of in regular bins.

  • Furniture and appliances
  • Construction materials
  • Metal scraps
  • Large household items

Electronic Waste

Discarded electronic devices and equipment.

  • Computers and laptops
  • Mobile phones
  • TVs and monitors
  • Other electronic devices

Hazardous Waste

Materials that pose potential risks to health and environment.

  • Chemicals and solvents
  • Paints and thinners
  • Batteries
  • Medical waste

Construction Waste

Materials generated during construction, renovation, or demolition.

  • Concrete and bricks
  • Wood and timber
  • Metal and steel
  • Construction debris
